Robyn Sue Fisher, Founder Smitten Ice Cream<

In 2009, Robyn Sue Fisher, a lifelong ice cream freak with an MBA from Stanford University, started selling her Smitten Ice Cream out of a Radio Flyer wagon in San Francisco.

Today, Fisher presides over a half-dozen Smitten Ice Cream shops in the Bay Area and Los Angeles with more than 120 employees.
